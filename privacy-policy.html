Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CursorConnector ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when merchants install and use the App.

1. Information We Collect

When you install the App, we may collect the following information:

  • Your Shopify store domain
  • Authentication data required to securely connect to your store (OAuth access token)
  • App configuration settings saved by you (such as CSS selectors, visual settings, and related preferences)

The App does not collect or process customer data, orders, payment information, or personally identifiable information of your customers.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the functionality of the App
  • Store and retrieve your configuration settings
  • Authenticate secure communication with Shopify
  • Improve app performance and reliability

3. Data Storage

App configuration data may be stored using Shopify's infrastructure (such as metafields) and/or secure application databases necessary to operate the App.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share merchant data with third parties.

Data may only be processed by trusted service providers strictly necessary for operating the App (such as hosting providers).

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the App’s functionality.

When you uninstall the App, access tokens are revoked and associated data may be deleted in accordance with Shopify’s data retention requirements.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your store data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
